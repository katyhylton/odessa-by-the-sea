Odessa by the Sea is your source for the best in Old Orchard Beach Maine lodging. Come to relax, or be as busy as you choose and enjoy seven uninterrupted miles of sandy beaches. Getaway to the Seaside

Take a stroll on the beach to The Pier or to Ocean Park and savor a variety of cuisine, enjoy the fireworks on the beach each Thursday evening, browse the shops, or sit back and watch the entertainment of Old Orchard Beach, Maine. You’ll find classic seaside amusements, deep sea fishing, whale watching, golf, shops and much more, in addition to the 7 miles of sandy beach waiting for you. Old Orchard Beach is also within a short drive to Portland, Freeport, Kennebunkport, and Ogunquit – making Old Orchard Beach the perfect Maine destination.

Weddings & Groups
